• Use mouse for tooltips and targeting
  • Esc - exit menu/show main menu
  • i - show inventory
  • g - pick up item
  • d - drop item
  • r - remove equipped item
  • ←, h - go/attack left
  • →, l - go/attack right
  • ↑, k - go/attack up
  • ↓, j - go/attack down
  • y - go/attack up-left
  • u - go/attack up-right
  • b - go/attack down-left
  • n - go/attack down-right
  • space - wait turn
  • . - descend stairs
